Additive & Subtractive Color

Early photgraphy utilized film, which relied on traditional, subtractive color models. Modern digital cameras rely on additive color models.

In a nutshell, in subtractive color, when colors are combined, form black. While on the other hand, additive colors when combined form white, or to be technical, light.
